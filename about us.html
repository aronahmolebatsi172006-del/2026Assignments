Background information

Arona's Coffee Shop is a small independent coffee shop located in Ramotswa, Gaborone, Botswana. We are a modern, friendly and convenient coffee shop built for our community. Our mission is to serve high quality manmade coffees and freshly baked pastries in a cozy, welcoming environment. We welcome everyone, from local residents to outside visitors, from teenagers to young professionals and beyond. Come in, place your order or order from home and pick up at the shop.We look forward to serving you.

Location

We are located in Ramotswa, Gaborone, Botswana. Pay us a visit or order online from the comfort of your home and collect your order when ready. We accept payment at the shop during pickup.
